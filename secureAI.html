SecureAI

SecureAI is a browser-based privacy tool that gives users direct insight and control over how their personal data is handled by AI applications. The tool detects whether AI models are processing sensitive information on websites in real time, empowering users to take action through a simple and intuitive interface.

Key Features

1. Real-Time Privacy Detection
Automatically scans active web sessions and flags when AI models interact with personal or sensitive data.
2. Interactive Browser Plugin
Seamlessly integrates with your browser to provide real-time updates and alerts without disrupting workflow.
3. Local-Only Data Processing
Ensures user data is never sent to the cloud. All analysis and inference happen locally on the user’s device.
4. Transparent AI Behavior Tracking
Logs interactions where AI tools might be reading, storing, or sharing user information — giving users full visibility.
5. Open-Source and Auditable
Fully available on GitHub to encourage community feedback, security reviews, and transparency.
6. Customizable Privacy Rules
Users can define and adjust their own privacy thresholds and filtering rules to suit different browsing contexts.
7. Lightweight & Fast
Built for performance with minimal overhead. SecureAI runs smoothly in the background, even on low-resource devices.
